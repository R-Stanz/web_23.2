Fry eggs

This a simple recipe, anyone can do it!

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tea spoon of butter
  • 0.5 mg of salt

Instructions:

  1. Take the tea spoon of oil and put it on a pan.
  2. Break the egg and put it on the pan.
  3. Let the egg rest for 2 min on the pan.
  4. Turn the egg around, while trying not to break it.
  5. Let the egg rest for another min on the pan.
